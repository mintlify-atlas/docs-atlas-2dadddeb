## Get Your First Quiz in 2 Minutes

This guide will walk you through creating your first AI-powered quiz on inspir. You'll go from signup to completed quiz in under 2 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Visit [quiz.inspir.uk](https://quiz.inspir.uk) and click **Create Account** in the top navigation.

    Fill in your details:
    - Email address
    - Username
    - Password (minimum 8 characters)

    <Note>
      **No credit card required** - All tools are available immediately after signup.
    </Note>

    After creating your account, you'll be automatically logged in and redirected to the dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Quiz Generator">
    From the homepage, you have several ways to start:
    
    - Click the **"Try Quiz Generator"** button in the hero section
    - Search for "quiz" in the tool search bar
    - Scroll to the tools section and select **Quiz Generator** (📝 icon)

    You'll land on the quiz generation interface with a large text input area.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Tell Us What to Quiz You On">
    The simplest way to start is to just describe what you want to be quizzed on:

    <CodeGroup>
    ```text Simple Topic
    Quiz me on the American Revolution
    ```

    ```text Specific Level
    Test me on Photosynthesis for my Biology exam
    ```

    ```text Custom Context
    Spanish verb conjugations for beginners - focus on present tense
    ```
    </CodeGroup>

    Type your topic or paste any text content into the large text area. The AI will generate 10 contextual questions instantly.

    <Tip>
      **Want better questions?** Expand the optional context section to add your notes or upload documents (TXT, DOCX). The more context you provide, the more targeted the questions.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload Study Materials (Optional)">
    For more targeted questions based on your specific materials, click **"Want better questions?"** to expand the optional section.

    You can:

    **Option 1: Paste Your Notes**
    ```text Example Notes
    Copy and paste lecture notes, textbook chapters, articles, or any study material here.
    
    The AI will generate questions specifically from this content, ensuring they're 
    relevant to exactly what you're studying.
    ```

    **Option 2: Upload Documents**
    - Drag and drop or click to browse
    - Supported formats: TXT, DOCX, DOC
    - Max file size: 10MB
    - The filename automatically becomes your quiz topic

    <Warning>
      Files must be text-based (TXT or DOCX). PDF support is coming soon.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate Your Quiz">
    Click the **"Generate Quiz"** button. The AI (powered by Claude) will:

    1. Analyze your topic and any provided context
    2. Generate 10 relevant questions (mix of multiple choice and short answer)
    3. Create questions that test understanding, not just memorization

    <Info>
      **Generation time:** Typically 10-20 seconds depending on context length.
    </Info>

    You'll see a loading animation with the message "Generating Your Quiz..."
  </Step>

  <Step title="Take Your Quiz">
    Once generated, you'll see:
    
    - **Progress bar** showing Question X of 10
    - **Question type badge** (Multiple Choice or Short Answer)
    - **Navigation dots** at the bottom to jump between questions
    - **Previous/Next buttons** to move through questions

    ### Answering Questions

    **Multiple Choice Questions:**
    - Select one option (A, B, C, or D)
    - Selected option highlights in blue
    - Click navigation dots to jump to specific questions

    **Short Answer Questions:**
    - Type your answer in the text area
    - Answers are evaluated for key concepts, not exact wording
    - Partial credit is given for partially correct answers

    <Tip>
      You can skip questions and come back later. The navigation dots show which questions have been answered (white) vs. unanswered (gray).
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit and Review Results">
    After answering all questions, click **"Submit Quiz"**.

    If you have unanswered questions, you'll get a confirmation prompt asking if you want to submit anyway.

    ### Your Results Page Shows:

    - **Overall score** (percentage and X/10 format)
    - **Performance badge** (Excellent, Good, or Needs Improvement)
    - **Question-by-question breakdown**:
      - ✅ Correct answers highlighted in green
      - ❌ Incorrect answers highlighted in red
      - Your answer vs. correct answer comparison
      - Detailed explanations for each question

    ### What You Can Do Next:

    ```typescript Results Actions
    // Available on the results page:
    - Review Mistakes: See detailed explanations
    - Share Quiz: Get a shareable link for friends/classmates
    - Retake Quiz: Try again to improve your score
    - View History: See all your past quizzes
    - Create Another: Generate a new quiz on any topic
    ```

    <Card title="Share Your Quiz" icon="share">
      Click the **Share** button to get a link. Anyone with the link can take the quiz—no account required. Perfect for study groups!
    </Card>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common Workflows

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Quiz-First Exam Prep">
    The most popular workflow for exam preparation:

    1. Upload lecture notes or type a topic → Generate quiz
    2. Take the quiz and review what you missed
    3. Use **Doubt Solver** for questions you got wrong
    4. Generate a new quiz on weak areas
    5. Repeat until you score 90%+

    This implements active recall—the most effective study technique.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Pomodoro + Review">
    Combine focus and active recall:

    1. Use **Study Timer** for a 25-minute focused session
    2. Take notes during your session
    3. During your 5-minute break, generate a quick quiz
    4. Quiz yourself on what you just studied
    5. Review mistakes before the next Pomodoro

    This locks in learning immediately while it's fresh.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Unblock a Stuck Problem">
    When you hit a roadblock:

    1. Navigate to **Doubt Solver** from the tools menu
    2. Type your question or upload a photo of the problem
    3. Get step-by-step explanation
    4. Once you understand, generate a quiz on that concept
    5. Take the quiz to ensure you truly understand

    This ensures you don't just memorize the answer—you learn the concept.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Tips for Better Quizzes

<Tip>
  **Be Specific:** Instead of "quiz me on biology," try "quiz me on cellular respiration for AP Biology - focus on the Krebs cycle and electron transport chain."
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Add Context:** Upload your actual lecture notes or textbook excerpts for questions that match exactly what you're studying.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Use the Subject Field:** When expanding optional context, select the subject from the dropdown for more accurate question types.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Review Explanations:** Don't just look at your score. Read the explanations for questions you got right too—you'll often learn something new.
</Tip>
