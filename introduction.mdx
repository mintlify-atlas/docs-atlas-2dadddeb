## What You Can Do

inspir provides a complete suite of study tools across multiple categories:

### Active Learning
Turn passive reading into active recall with:
- **Quiz Generator** - Upload PDFs, DOCX, or paste text to generate AI-powered quizzes instantly
- **Flashcard Creator** - Create and study with AI-generated flashcards
- **Practice Test Builder** - Build custom practice tests from your study materials
- **Mind Map Creator** - Create visual mind maps to organize ideas
- **Concept Map Builder** - Build concept maps to connect related ideas

### AI Help
Get instant, intelligent assistance 24/7:
- **Doubt Solver** - Get step-by-step solutions to any homework question (text or image)
- **Math Solver** - Solve math problems with detailed explanations
- **Text Summarizer** - Condense long texts into key points
- **Study Guide Generator** - Generate comprehensive study guides from notes
- **Citation Generator** - Generate citations in APA, MLA, Chicago, and Harvard styles

### Focus & Productivity
Stay focused and productive:
- **Study Timer** - Pomodoro timer with custom intervals
- **Custom Timer** - Flexible timer for any task
- **Focus Mode** - Distraction-free study sessions
- **Break Reminder** - Automatic break notifications
- **Deep Work Sessions** - Track deep work time

### Gamification
Build lasting habits with motivation:
- **Study Streaks** - Track daily study activity (🔥 streaks)
- **XP & Leveling** - Earn experience points and level up
- **Badges** - Unlock achievement badges
- **Leaderboards** - Compete with other learners
- **Challenges** - Complete weekly study challenges
- **Daily Goals** - Set and track daily targets

### Organization
Keep your academic life organized:
- **Cornell Notes** - Structured note-taking system with PDF export
- **Grade Calculator** - Calculate grades and predict scores
- **GPA Tracker** - Track your GPA across semesters
- **Study Planner** - Plan your study sessions
- **Assignment Tracker** - Never miss a deadline
- **Course Manager** - Organize courses and materials

### Social Learning
Connect and collaborate:
- **Student Forum** - Connect with students, share resources, ask questions
- **Study Groups** - Form study groups and collaborate
- **Resource Sharing** - Share notes and materials

## The inspir Difference

<Note>
  **Powered by Claude AI** - inspir uses Anthropic's Claude API to generate contextual questions, provide detailed explanations, and adapt to your learning style.
