## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an Account">
    Sign up at [quiz.inspir.uk](https://quiz.inspir.uk) to save your progress across all tools
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Your Tools">
    Start with the tools that match your current needs - whether it's creating a quiz, getting homework help, or starting a focus timer
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Build Your Routine">
    Use multiple tools together: generate flashcards, study with the timer, track your progress, and check your grades
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  All tools work without an account, but signing up saves your history, enables sharing, and unlocks gamification features.
</Note>
